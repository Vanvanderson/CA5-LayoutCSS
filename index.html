Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 180g
  • 1 1/2 cup butter unsalted, room temp, 113g
  • 1 cup peanut butter, 250g
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar 100g, lightly packed
  • 1/2 cup sugar 100g
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract 5mL
  • 1 egg large, room temp
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder 3g

Appliances and Tools

  • Oven
  • Stand Mixer
  • Measuring Cups
  • Fork
  • Baking Sheet
  • Parchment Paper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Sift flour and baking powder into a large bowl then whisk to combine.
  2. Cream butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar in a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment. Add peanut butter and mix until incorporated.
  3. Mix in the room-temperature egg and vanilla extract, then scrape the bowl and mix again.
  4. Add flour mixture and beat on low until just incorporated.
  5. Roll the cookie dough into one-inch balls and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Flatten the cookie dough balls with a fork in a criss-cross pattern. Bake cookies for about 8 minutes. Allow cookies to cool completely on a baking sheet, they need to set up before being transferred.
